Target Audience Scenarios:

It is a joke commonly used amongst us brown ones that on the hottest day, our moms make soup.

This was the main inspiration for the purpose of this site. I don’t cook now that I live by myself (because CLEAN UP, HELLO!!)however, before that, I would base the day’s menu on the weather, although living in Houston, that was a bit complicated.

Cloudywithachanceofpreston.com; is targeted to:

  • Lunch ladies or other cooks for big crowds
  • Party planners or caterers
  • Mothers who plan on cooking for their families
  • Anyone cooking interested in avoiding a kitchen faux pass
  • Menu planners in any scale
