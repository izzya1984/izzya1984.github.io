Overview
onceuponadork.com

A Unicorn and a Mermaid... making each other laugh

This site is a fun way to interact with potential clients of our comedy act.
Have you ever looked at your best friend and wish the world would be a witness to your hilarious conversations? This site is created with that in mind.

I can’t count the many song parodies, funny hashtags, jokes, musicals, nicknames, etcetera, that Michelle and I had talked about in our short 2 or so year old friendship, it hasn’t been a long friendship, but is full of quality. As we call ourselves inspired by one of our favorite shows: Rainbow Infused Space Unicorn (Michelle) & Poetic Noble Land Mermaid (Itzel).

This site will display jokes, parodies, stories, and random funny things. It will also include a contact form to book a comedy/stand up/ or other funny services needed.

Target Audience

Since most of our jokes are based in pop culture, and things growing up, our target audience is very limited to people we can relate to, did I mention we are both single?

Most of our jokes work around Jane Austen literature, 80's trivia, and how clueless guys can be, I would target women in the Mid-Single community. Age 30-50.
