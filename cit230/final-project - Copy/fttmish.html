Full Time Temple Missionaries

Senior couples continue to be a "much-needed asset" in LDS missionary work, said Elder Quentin L. Cook, a member of the First Quorum of the Seventy and the executive director of the church's missionary department. At any given time, there are about 2,200 couples serving full-time missions around the world, a number that has increased from the 1,600 serving a decade ago. They make up about 4 percent of the total missionary force. And LDS leaders say there is a constant need for more couples to serve.

At Temple Inn & Suites, we offer affordable long term accommodations for Senior missionary couples. Our Full Time Missionary bungalows come with a fully equipped kitchen and are fully furnished to provide comfort during your time of service.
