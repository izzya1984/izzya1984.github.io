Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

WDD 331 Group 14 Members: Itzel Garcia, Brian Kent, Steven Philips

Objective: Apply a CSS them to HTML content set by Zen Garden

Introduction: Our group kicked around a number of really cool ideas and then weighed them out based on aesthetics, practicality, and capabilities based on time constraints. As we discussed these principles this narrowed the scope to focus on our theme, layout, potential additional content, and wireframing.

Theme: We got excited when we decided on the colorful theme of “Candy”. Candy is bright, interesting in shapes and has a child like appeal for a fun flare. Instantly with candy as a theme you can picture different candies, packaging and stores where candy is sold. There is no shortage of ideas for displaying candy in bins, jars, coin-op dispensers, bins and wall hangers. Everyone in the world knows what candy is and immediately can relate in a nostalgic manner.
