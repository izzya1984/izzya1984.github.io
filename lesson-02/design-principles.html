Design Principles

Rule of Thirds

The main idea about the Rule of Thirds is to divide the layout into nine equal sections with the two horizontal and two vertical lines. Each of these sections will take about 33,33% of the horizontal space and the same percent of the vertical space. That's why they are called “Thirds”. In the CFA Mobile site is more about photography, but the same rule still applies

Contrast

A while back, during my typography class I was having some trouble with the color combinations. Best advice that I received from my instructor regarding that was “print it on black and white, if you can’t tell the difference, change your colors.” In the Zappos Site mobile and desktop the contrast on the information, as well as the photography, make everything easy to read, and too easy to shop too ( ask my credit card)
